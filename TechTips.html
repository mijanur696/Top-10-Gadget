How to disable pen drive in windows PC

Step 01

First open the run box (window button+R) then write down here regedit then open up a window. You will see this kind of window.

Step 02

Then click HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE

Step 03

Then click SYSTEM

Step 04

Then click CurrentControlset

Step 05

Then click Services

Step 06

Then search USBSTOR, click the USBSTOR then click the start

Step 07

Then change the Hexadecimal value 3 to 4 Now deactive your USB ports
